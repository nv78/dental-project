
# dental project

Dental Treatment Financing Calculator 


PROJECT: Your goal is to build a dental treatment financing calculator, which should allow the office manager to input the treatment cost and insurance details to give the patient an instant breakdown of cost. 

A dental clinic front desk inputs:
Treatment cost (fixed at $1,000 for this project)
Insurance coverage percentage (slider input: 0% to 100%)
The system should calculate:
Insurance covered amount (displayed as a dollar amount)
Out-of-pocket amount (displayed as a dollar amount)
Note: the breakdown must update instantly as the slider moves
For the out of pocket amount, there should be multiple options for the patient to choose from including:
Pay the out of pocket portion in full
Pay the out of pocket portion in 3 monthly installments
Pay the out of pocket portion in 6 monthly installments
Pay the out of pocket portion in 12 monthly installments
All currency must be rounded to exactly two decimal places.
